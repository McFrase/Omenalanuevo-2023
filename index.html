Omenala Nuevo: Where Branding, Exhibitions, and Logistics Come Together to Make Magic!

Welcome To Omenala Nuevo

A young budding and award winning exhibition, branding and logistics company in Nigerian, located in Lagos State, which exists because of her passion for exhibition and corporate branding. Here, we see and believe in new ways of visualizing and birthing new ideas of conceptualization and service delivery and we are driven by passion to make a significant difference, taking events from concepts to production.

A place for every brand

We believe excellence is never an accident, it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, and intelligent execution.

Exhibitions

At Omenala Nuevo, we have realized that exhibitions are one of the quickest and most cost-effective means of exploring and entering new markets for brands and companies, this is because there is nothing more reassuring to customers than doing business face-to-face.

Logistics

At Omenala Nuevo, our logistics process involves the integration of information flow, materials handling, production, packaging, inventory, transportation, warehousing, and often security.

Event Management

At Omenala Nuevo, what we do is to study your brand, identifying its target audience, devising the event concept, and coordinating the technical aspects before actually launching your event.

Corporate Branding

At Omenala Nuevo, we realize that consumers typically recognize and remember visual expressions more powerfully than they remember names or words. This is what we do for your brand.

Event Kits Rental

At Omenala Nuevo, we realize that no two exhibitions or promotional events are the same, at such, we have put together for rentals a variety of packages in their different types and sizes, to suit your events, exhibition and promotional requirements.
