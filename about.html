About Us

Creating happiness, just for you

A young budding and award winning exhibition, branding and logistics company in Nigerian, located in Lagos State, which exists because of her passion for exhibition and corporate branding. Here, we see and believe in new ways of visualizing and birthing new ideas of conceptualization and service delivery and we are driven by passion to make a significant difference, taking events from concepts to production.

"Our mandate is to provide strategic sustainable service delivery across the value- chain of Brand and Event Activation, Exhibitions, Corporate Branding, Logistics and Warehousing."

Skilled at delivering dynamic branding solution for various industries

We believe that experience is everything – what we do at Omenala Nuevo is create an unforgettable experience with your brand for your target audience, thereby ensuring client’s brand expansion, and a more positive impact. Omenala Nuevo’s comprehensive range of services includes exhibit design and production, the design, planning and management of events, rentals, installation and dismantle services, coupled with a wide range of exhibition solutions as well as the full spectrum of branding solutions.
